Upload Multiple Datasets (Cross-Domain)

How Cross-Domain Knowledge Graph Works:
  • Upload at least 2 files from different domains
  • System extracts entities from each file separately
  • Finds relationships BETWEEN different domains
  • Example: "Einstein" (Science) → "Theory of Relativity" (Physics)
  • Admin can approve/reject cross-domain relations
Example Cross-Domain Pairs:
Science + Technology:
"Quantum Computing" + "Artificial Intelligence"
Healthcare + AI:
"Medical Diagnosis" + "Machine Learning"
Law + Business:
"Corporate Law" + "Startup Companies"
